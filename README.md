# Write-Off Calculator

Free tax calculators for W-2 employees with a side business (LLC). See what business expenses actually cost you after tax savings.

**Live at [writeoff-calc.vercel.app](https://writeoff-calc.vercel.app)**

## Calculators

- **Write-Off Calculator** — Enter a business purchase and see the real price after your tax write-off
- **Home Office Costs** — See the real monthly cost of rent, internet, and utilities after your home office deduction
- **Quarterly Estimates** — Calculate how much to set aside each quarter when you have W-2 income and an LLC

## Privacy

Your data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
